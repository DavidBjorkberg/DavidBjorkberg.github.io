PC to mobile port
Unreal Engine 5 Optimization

For a couple months at Piktiv I worked on a project where we ported a PC open world game to mobile. 

The primary focus of this project was to optimize it enough so it ran well on mobile. Since this was a PC game with no previous plans of going mobile, a lot needed to be optimized.

Even though I only worked on it for a few months, I learned a lot about profiling, what is expensive and how to optimize it.

My job was to look for expensive areas in the game, find out what's expensive and then find and implement a solution to it. The solution could be anything from a complex system to simply removing the expensive element.


What I learned

- How to profile in Unreal Engine
- What's expensive for the GPU/CPU/Memory
