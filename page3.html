DirectX Renderer

Graphics renderer built from the ground up in DirectX and HLSL. 
Techniques:
    Phong shading (Ambient,Diffuse,Specular)
    Deferred rendering
    Displacement mapping
    Height mapping
    Camera movement with WASD
    Normal mapping
    Front to back rendering
    Back-face culling
    Water animation through UV-manipulation
    Mirror
The purpose of showing this project in my portfolio is to display knowledge of writing shaders in HLSL.
