Who am I?

My name is David Björkberg and I am a team oriented programmer who strives to write clean, scalable code.
I first gained interest in programming when I started high school, where I learned C# and Unity over a 3 year period.
I then started University at Blekinge Tekniska Högskola where I learned C++, DirectX, scripting, git, how to work on a game as a team, etc.
Towards the end of my 3rd year of University, I was hired as an intern for a small game studio to develop prototypes in Unity that they could pitch to their investors.
This internship was forced to end after 3 months because of COVID-19, but during this time I learned a lot about working in a professional workflow and how to implement someone elses vision of a game, instead of just my own.
