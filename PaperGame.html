Paper Game

(Android / IOS)

At Attractive Interactive, I worked on Paper Game, a game where players create levels by taking pictures with their phone. Draw a level on paper, snap a picture and play instantly, or capture anything around you and turn it into a 2D Platformer level.

What I learned:
- How to optimize in Unity
- Collaborating in a game development team
- Writing sustainable code

Contributions:
- Optimized Menu
- Created new placeable enemies
- Water shader
